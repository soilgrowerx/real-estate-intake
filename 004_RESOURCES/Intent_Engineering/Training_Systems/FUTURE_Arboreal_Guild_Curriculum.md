## The Directive
The user wants to create an AI prompt to generate a **Comprehensive Arborist Training Taskbook**. It must cover: **Safety & Compliance, Tree Biology, Climbing & Rigging**, and specific specialized modules (PHC, Utility).

Your mission is to architect the **Master Prompt** that the user will feed to the AI. This Master Prompt must force the AI to generate highly specific, measurable, and safe training tasks (JPRs - Job Performance Requirements).

2.  **Skill Progression:** The curriculum must move from **Groundie** (Chipping, Roping) -> **Climber** (Ascent, Positioning) -> **Crew Leader** (Assessment, Rescue).
3.  **Specific Standards:** Reference **ANSI Z133** (Safety) and **A300** (Management) standards explicitly.
