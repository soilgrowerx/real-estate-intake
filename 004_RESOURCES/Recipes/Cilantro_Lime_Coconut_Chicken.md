# Recipe: Cilantro Lime Coconut Chicken


# Cilantro Lime Coconut Chicken

## Ingredients (Store List)
- Coconut creme (1 can)
- Big pack of chicken thighs
- Cilantro (1 handful)
- Limes (2 big ones)
- Fresh garlic (5 cloves, diced)
- Salt
- Pepper
- Paprika
- Olive oil
- Vegetable broth (1/4 cup)

## Instructions
1. Stick can of coconut creme in warm water.
2. Heat olive oil in pan on low/medium.
3. Sprinkle a SMALL amount of salt and pepper on chicken thighs.
4. Start searing chicken. Sprinkle other side of chicken with salt, pepper and paprika (lightly).
5. Flip chicken after a few minutes.
6. When almost done searing, add 5 diced fresh garlic cloves.
7. Once done searing, add 1 warm can coconut cream, handful of cilantro, squeeze 2 (big) limes and like a quarter cup of vegetable broth.
8. Stir around a little and put a lid on it. Cook on low while rice cooks.
