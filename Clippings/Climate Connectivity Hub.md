## How to get started

## How do I begin?

Glad you asked! To get started, simply watch the video or follow the step-by-step instructions below.

![](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=xozHPfzDPfo)

## Getting started – step by step

### Explore

- Click on the crosshair of any search result. You will be able to explore that search topic in the context of other connected articles, projects, organizations. The platform source for named articles is indicated on the outside of the blue circles.
- Or in Explorer view, use a search term to see what content is available.
![Explore functionality](https://connectivity-hub.weadapt.org/images/getting-started-explore.png)

### Filter

- Refine the search if needed by specific a country or location for example.
- You can also filter the results by platform or resource type:
- **Keywords** – terms from the Climate Connectivity Taxonomy, an expertly curated glossary of climate vocabulary. Click on them to explore definitions, related terms, and scope notes.
- **Articles** – resources from European and global climate and disaster risk platforms, including case studies, blogs, reports, event summaries, and more.
- **Projects** – EU-funded research and development projects from the CORDIS database, focusing on climate neutrality, sustainable energy, climate science, and adaptation.
- **Organizations** – contributors to projects and articles. Find out who is working on what!
![Filter functionality](https://connectivity-hub.weadapt.org/images/getting-started-filter.png)

### Read more

- The 'Read more' button at the bottom of any content window takes you to the full content on the source platform, such as the Resilience Platform, PreventionWeb, CORDIS or weADAPT.
![Read more functionality](https://connectivity-hub.weadapt.org/images/getting-started-read-more.png)
