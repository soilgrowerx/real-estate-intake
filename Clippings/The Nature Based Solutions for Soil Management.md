![](https://nbsoil.eu/wp-content/uploads/2023/05/NBSOIL-Nature-based-Solutions-horizontal-logo-mail.png)**Healthy soil sustains life on Earth** — it feeds us, supports nature, and helps fight climate change. **Yet it’s often overlooked in policy and public debate**. NBSOIL is changing that by putting soil health back at the centre of the discussions.

Through an innovative blended learning programme, **NBSOIL is training a new generation of soil advisors and providing tools and resources** to put nature-based solutions (NBS) into action for sustainable soil management.

![](https://nbsoil.eu/wp-content/uploads/2025/11/about.jpg)

The NBSOIL Academy is a pioneering **training initiative designed to empower both new and experienced soil advisors** to put nature-based solutions into practice. It offers a rich **blend of theoretical and practical learning**, equipping you with the knowledge and skills to support sustainable soil management.

Yes! The academy offers an accessible and well-structured platform to explore the interconnectedness of agriculture, ecosystems, and soil health. It presents scientific information in a clear and simple way, making it accessible even for those without a formal academic background in soil science. I found it particularly useful for gaining practical insights into EU policies and environmental initiatives.

NBSOIL focuses on six multifunctional practices to test them as NBS to improve soil health in Europe. The knowledge base showcases a portfolio of these practices for soil health addressing the EU Mission Soil objectives.

![](https://nbsoil.eu/wp-content/uploads/2025/11/kwnoledge.png) ![](https://nbsoil.eu/wp-content/uploads/2025/11/demo-girl-full.png)

Across Poland, Austria, Italy, Spain, and Switzerland, project sites bring nature-based solutions (NBS) to life, showing how soil health can be restored. They offer a hands-on look at the tools, knowledge, and techniques needed to make the NBS central to the project work in practice.

NBSOIL gives soil advisors and farmers the tools to assess, understand, and improve soil health — from the Health Index Tool to ARIES Open Library or a GIS tool. Science-backed insights, ready for action.
