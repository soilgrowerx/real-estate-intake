## Rule Summary

The Clean Power Plan established emission guidelines for states to follow in limiting carbon dioxide (CO <sub>2</sub>) emissions from existing power plants.  EPA repealed the CPP in June 2019 and replaced it with the [Affordable Clean Energy rule](https://www.epa.gov/stationary-sources-air-pollution/affordable-clean-energy-rule).
