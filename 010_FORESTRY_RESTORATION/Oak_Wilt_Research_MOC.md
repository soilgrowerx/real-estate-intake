# Oak Wilt & Soil Biome Research
**Project:** Soil Grower / Arboracle Research
**Topic:** Novel treatments, hormonal regulation, and xylem pathology.

## Core Hypotheses
- **Hormonal Regulation:** Brassinosteroids (from Smilax) as xylem-defense modulators.
- **Tylosis Inhibition:** Amino acid pathway regulation (Methionine/Tryptophan) to prevent vascular occlusion in Live Oaks.
- **Antagonist Signaling:** Salicylic acid as a jasmonate-antagonist to block ethylene-triggered tylosis.

## Research Findings (2025-2026)
- **Jasmonates + Ethylene:** Synergistic trigger for tyloses (Leśniewska et al 2017).
- **Salicylic Acid:** Abolishes jasmonate-induced tyloses.
- **AVG (Aminoethoxyvinylglycine):** Ethylene biosynthesis inhibitor; sourced from Streptomyces fermentation.
- **Phytotoxicity:** High hormone concentrations (brassinolide) risk systemic damage during injection.
