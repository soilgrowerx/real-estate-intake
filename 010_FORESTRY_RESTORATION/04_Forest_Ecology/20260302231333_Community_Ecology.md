What Can We Calculate with Our 
Measurements
 Density (D) & Relative Density (RD)

--- Page 27 ---

Density
Density:
Di = Number of individual trees of species “i”/Total area 
sampled
Relative Density:
RDi = Density of species “i” / Sum of all densities

--- Page 28 ---

Frequency
Frequency:
fi = Number of quadrats species “i” is in / Total number of 
quadrats sampled
Relative Frequency:
Rfi = Frequency of species “i” / Sum of all frequencies

--- Page 29 ---

Basal Area
Basal Area
Bi = ∑ [pi * (DBH)2/4] 
Sample calculations use pi = 3.14
Relative Basal Area:
RBi = Sum of basal area of species “i” / Sum of all basal 
areas (all species)
