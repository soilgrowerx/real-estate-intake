FAQs about the accelerator program
22
What are the key dates?
The Accelerator program will begin in March 2025 and end after 12 weeks, in mid-June 2025.
What language is used during the programs for communication and content?
All programming will be conducted in English.
What time zone will programming be conducted in?
We anticipate founders working from many diverse world regions and time zones. We will do our best to accommodate 
convenience for a critical mass and generally anticipate most sessions being friendly to the relatively central CET time zone.
What is the expected time commitment?
At least one of the founders must attend each week’s live coaching session, which ranges from 60-90 minutes. Additionally, 
team members are invited to attend business- and technology- speciﬁc virtual mentoring or expert sessions each week.
Please note that active participation will be a requirement to unlock stipend payments.
What role does the product-market ﬁt play in the Accelerator program?
Climate Collective has partnered with climate-tech PMF expert Peter Nocchiero, who will lead 6 of the 12 weeks of thematic 
programming and coaching. Learn more here.

FAQs about the accelerator program
23
What’s the difference between a coach, a mentor, and an expert?
Coaches lead each week’s thematic session and are available for 1:1 support during the week about that session’s theme. The 
Coaches for the Accelerator have already been identiﬁed. Think of them as a mix of a professor and a football coach - they bring 
expertise but will also help you progress and incorporate their expertise into your work.
Mentors are individuals who will be paired with teams throughout their time in the Accelerator, based on team needs and 
mentor availability. Think of them as an established player in the ecosystem who can be a sounding board and offer guidance 
and support as needed. Mentors will be available for 1:1 support. 
Experts are guest speakers who will share insights on speciﬁc topics that may or may not be related to the week’s thematic 
session. Experts will be determined based on the needs of Accelerator participants. We're locking in representatives from 
world-class companies and thought-leaders. Their expertise will add to your unfair advantage.
How does the stipend work?
Stipends of about $10,000 USD will be available for each participating team. Think of this as a contribution to the time you will 
dedicate to the accelerator.
Teams will enter in a contractual agreement with Climate Collective to articulate speciﬁc participation-based milestones that are 
required in order to unlock tranches of stipend payments.

FAQs about the accelerator program
24
How can we get funded after the Accelerator program concludes?
We provide small stipends to support your participation in the accelerator, but you may well need more funding to really make 
it. The program concludes with Founders Week: a set of activations that celebrate participants’ progress, amplify their work, and 
connect them with potential investors. The organizers have a vast network of VC, investor, and philanthropic stakeholders who 
will be invited for activities during Founders Week like 1:1 meetings with any teams who are ready for a funding conversation.
We’ll coach you so you’re as effective as possible, and do everything we can to help get you follow on funding.
What other support or resources can we get after the Accelerator concludes?
Climate Collective is part of a vast network of climate technology stakeholders. After the accelerator concludes, you are part of 
that community and can expect access to community events and connection opportunities with this broader network.

FAQs about the application and eligibility
25
Can we apply if we have an idea only?
Yes. In cases of idea-only submissions, we will pay extra attention to signals in your application that you have the operational 
and subject-matter expertise to build your idea.
Can we apply if we are pre-revenue or early-stage?
Yes. Early-stage startups are welcome. Even if you don’t have revenue or a product, you will ﬁnd opportunities in your 
application to express where you are receiving traction, or how you know your idea uniquely addresses a real problem.
Can we apply if we have received Series B funding?
This Accelerator will accept projects and startups that have received up to Series A funding. Startups at more advanced funding 
stages will not be a good ﬁt.
Can we apply if we are already part of another accelerator?
Yes, as long as you disclose these affiliations in the corresponding section of the application.
Can we apply if we have previously received funding or support from other programs?
Yes, as long as you disclose this funding in the corresponding section of the application.

FAQs about the application and eligibility
26
Are we required to share IP or sensitive data as part of the program?
No. You will always be in charge of what you share with the program stakeholders. However, keep in mind that limiting access to 
certain information, such as customer acquisition data during MVP or PMF tests, will prevent you from participating fully in the 
program.
I am a solo founder. Can I apply?
Yes, but we will look to understand your team strategy, or goals for building a team around your idea.
Can I update my application after submission?
No, once you submit your application, it is considered ﬁnal.
Can we apply from any country?
Yes, as long as your business complies with US sanctions regulations.

FAQs about the application and eligibility
27
I don’t have a team video. Can I still apply?
A team video is a required part of the application. You should not worry about production value and focus instead on answering 
the application’s prompts succinctly.
The two-minute maximum is strictly followed. Reviewers will stop watching after 2 minutes of the video.
If you and your cofounders don’t live in the same place to ﬁlm a video, you can ﬁlm half each and splice them together, or 
record a videoconference. Your entrepreneurial journey will encounter plenty of bigger obstacles to overcome, so don’t let this 
be a barrier!
When will I hear back about my application?
We will notify all valid applicants of their status by March 2025.
Do I need to have a business plan to apply?
No. The application will ask for your brief business model ideas or progress, but a full business plan is not needed.
