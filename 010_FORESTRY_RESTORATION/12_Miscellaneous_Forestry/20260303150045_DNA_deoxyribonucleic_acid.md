RNA – ribonucleic acid
• Replaces thymine (T) with uracil (U)
• Single stranded
• Has a 5’ cap and a 3’ poly (A) tail
– Protect from degradation
– Cap is a G hooked on backwards
– Poly (A) tail is about 200 A nucleotides
• Starts in nucleus and ends in cytoplasm

• Ribosome
• Reads mRNA in codons, going 5’ to 3’
– AUG – start; UAG, UAA, UGA - stop
• Codons = amino acids
• 20 amino acids
• tRNAs deliver amino acids to ribosome
• String of amino acids folds to form protein
