*Core Themes:*

1. **Diagnostics & Treatment**: Understanding the causes and effects of tree health issues is central to effective management.
2. **Knowledge of Conditions**: A thorough knowledge of both biotic (living organisms) and abiotic (non-living factors) conditions impacting trees.
3. **Systematic Approach**: Employing a systematic process for diagnosis, from observation to logical analysis.
4. **Correct Identification**: Accurately identifying the species of the host tree and any pests or diseases is crucial.
