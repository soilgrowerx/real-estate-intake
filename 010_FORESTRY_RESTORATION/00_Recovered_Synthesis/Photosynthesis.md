### Photosynthesis
• CO2 + H2O + energy 🡪 CH2O + O2
### • 12CO2 + 11H2O + 24 NADPH + 36 ATP 🡪
### C12H22O11 + 12O2
• Light reaction
• Produces energy (2 NADPH and 3 ATP) from light and water
(splits 2 H2O and uses 4 e-)
• ATP – adenosine tri-phosphate
• NADPH - Nicotinamide adenine dinucleotide phosphate
• Dark reaction
• Produces sugar and O2 from energy, CO2, and H2O
