### ISA Arborist Certification
### The arborist certification process has 2
steps; make sure to allow enough time to
complete both steps:
### Apply online with ISA for approval to
take the exam.
(up to 10 days to process)
### After receiving approval, enroll in the
exam location of your choice.
(enrollment must be at least 12
business days in advance)
### Recommendations:
• Download and read the Certification Application Guide. This is where the requirements and form
to provide to ISA for eligibility.
• Create a personal ISA account.
o This allows you to get approved online and prove eligibility.
o This provides an easy wat to enroll in an exam of your choice. Be prepared to pay in
advance of taking the exam.
o This is where you find your personal CEU history and credential status.
o Find an exam by going to this link after you have logged into your account. This is where
to find the “live” paper-based exams.
o Find an exam by going to this link for electronic exams. This is through PearsonVue
testing centers.
• Join the Indiana Arborist Association (IAA) to learn more about arborist certification preparation
courses and exams.
Questions? Contact the IAA office for additional information.
info@indiana-arborist.org or call 219-295-0048
