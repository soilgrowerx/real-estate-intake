### Respiration
### • C12H22O11 + 12O2 🡪 12CO2 + 11H2O + ATP
• Glycolysis - cytoplasm
• Produces 4 ATP, 4 NADH, and 4 pyruvates (3C) from sucrose
• Krebs Cycle – matrix of mitochondria
• Produces 4 ATP, 4 FADH2, 16 NADH and 12 CO2 from 4 pyruvates
• Electron Transport Chain – inner membrane of mitochondria
• Produces ATP from FADH2 and NADH
