### Why Bank Seeds?
### Reforestation is dependent on
supplies of viable seeds from
appropriate species and locations –
this is one of the largest
bottlenecks in scaling up
restoration projects.
León-Lobos et al. (2020). Restoration Ecology, 28(2), 277–281.
Merritt & Dixon. (2011). Science, 332(6028), 424–425.
### Rapid Response
### Disasters like wildfires and hurricanes
can devastate landscapes, and many
native species are outcompeted by
invasive plants without restoration. If
native seeds are not banked for
emergency response, revegetation with
non-native seeds can put ecosystems at
further risk.
Trauernicht et al. (2018). Forest Ecology and Management, 411, 1–11.
Van Beusekom et al. (2018). Remote Sensing, 10(9), 1386.
