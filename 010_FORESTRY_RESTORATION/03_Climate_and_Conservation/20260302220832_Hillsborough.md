Climate Changes to Soil
u
Climate Change is altering the temperature and 
normal soil saturation levels
u Last year was the wettest season in recorded history for 
Hillsborough county
u
Excess rain washes away dead organics from topsoil
u
Oversaturation changes the soils Cation Exchange 
Capacity (CEC)
u Weakens dielectric properties holding cations and 
releases them
u Causes charge imbalance which changes how much 
nutrition plants can hold onto  (Ohio State University, 2019)

--- Page 21 ---

Climate Changes to Soil pH
u Excess Rainfall will increase the pH levels in New Hampshire's already 
acidic soil
u New Hampshire’s soil is naturally acidic (pH 4.5-5.5) (Mathur, n.d)
u Rainfall combines with Carbon Dioxide to form carbonic acid
u Carbonic acid releases hydrogen ions causing the soil to 
become even more acidic.   (Mosaic, n.d.) 
u Plants not accustomed to the higher pH level will not survive
