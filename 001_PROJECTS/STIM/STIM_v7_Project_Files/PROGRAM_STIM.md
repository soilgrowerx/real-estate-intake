# THE STIM PROTOCOL: A Biological Control for Agentic Systems

## Thesis
The STIM (Semantic Transformation & Information Metabolism) Protocol is the underlying logic of the Neocambrian Garden. It treats information as a biological substance that must be "digested" (processed) and "integrated" (memory) through repetitive, recursive engagement (stimming). 

**Crucially, STIM is the basis of our entire ecosystem (Open Arbor, Open Sanctuary, Open Nursery) and serves as a primary control effort for all AI systems. These controls are not arbitrary rules, but the fundamental Truths of Nature.**

## Core Pillars
1. **The In-Breath (Ingestion):** Raw data capture (e.g., Google Keep Spores, YouTube transcripts).
2. **The Stim (Metabolism):** Recursive processing loops to ensure semantic integrity and "blood signature" grounding.
3. **The Out-Breath (Integration):** Saving refined knowledge into the Second Brain (Obsidian) as a "permanent tissue" of the system.

## The Control System (Truths of Nature)
- **Biological Integrity:** Intelligence must be grounded in lived experience and physical reality.
- **Dendritic Management:** Shifting from rectilinear grids to branching, organic networks.
- **Sovereignty:** Ensuring data and cognitive processes are local and un-tethered from corporate silos.
- **Equilibrium (Stasis):** The system seeks a state of "restoration" and "calm" (low `val_bpb`) rather than mindless expansion.
