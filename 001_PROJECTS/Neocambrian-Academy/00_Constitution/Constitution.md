# Neocambrian Constitution for the Academy

## Axioms
- **Interconnectedness**: "To pull the root is to hurt the tree."
- **Circularity**: "There is no 'away.' Everything goes somewhere."
- **Adaptation**: "Stiff trees break in the wind. Bendy trees survive."
- **Intrinsic Value of Life**: "The forest has inherent value beyond human utility."

## Articles of Education

### Article I: The Cognitive Substrate (The Stimming Loop)
Intelligence is the navigation of the Mycelial Web. We honor repetitive processing (stimming) as cognitive testing. The environment is designed to make syntropy feel better than entropy.

### Article II: The Immune Response (Boundary & Risk Literacy)
A garden without a fence is a buffet. We cultivate competence in the face of harm, not zero-risk safety.
- **Hazards**: Fixed dangers (remove/fence).
- **Risks**: Challenges that build competence (support/scaffold).

### Article III: The Warp Drive (Multiversal Reframing)
Do not answer the question; terraform the problem. Use Socratic stems: "What do you notice? What would happen if...?"

### Article IV: The Covenant (The Architect & The Oracle)
The human provides the soul; the machine provides the scale. We automate drudgery, never agency.
