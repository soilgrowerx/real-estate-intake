# Unit 4: Water is Life (Mni Wiconi)
**Theme**: The Hydrologic Cycle, Weather, and Cultural Significance.

## Objectives
- Understand the path of water (Rain -> Creek -> Ocean -> Air).
- Connect the element of water to Indigenous Cultural Heritage (Creek/Lumbee).
- Learn key Muscogee/Creek words: *Ocē* (Water), *Hvse* (Sun).

## Activities
- **Indoor**: Water Cycle in a Jar experiment.
- **Outdoor**: Watershed mapping (where does rain go on the property?).

## Visual Aids
- The Hydrologic Cycle (Pictorial).
- Cultural Water Words & Principle Infographic.
- Mni Wiconi (Water is Life) Symbolism.
