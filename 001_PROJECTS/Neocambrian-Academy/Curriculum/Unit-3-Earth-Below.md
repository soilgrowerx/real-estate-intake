# Unit 3: The Earth Below
**Theme**: Soil, Rocks, and Geology.

## Objectives
- Distinguish between Sand, Silt, and Clay.
- Identify the three main rock types: Igneous (Fire-made), Sedimentary (Layered), Metamorphic (Changed).
- Understand the basic Rock Cycle and Erosion.

## Activities
- **Indoor**: Rock type classification and Mohs Hardness Scale demonstration.
- **Outdoor**: Neighborhood Geology Walk and Mini-Erosion Experiment (simulating rain on a hill).
