# Neocambrian Academy: Unit 3 Expanded - The Earth Below
**Theme**: Soil, Rocks, and Geology.
**Target Age**: 3-5 Years

## I. Weekly Lesson Cycles

### Day 1: The Earth's Bones (Rock Types)
- **Indoor (Agent Thea)**: **Rock Sorting.** Present nine distinct samples (3 Igneous, 3 Sedimentary, 3 Metamorphic). Use color-coded trays (Red, Blue, Green) to sort them by "how they were made."
- **Outdoor (Agent Scout)**: **The Stone Scavenger Hunt.** Walk the property to find "Fire-Made" (shiny/glassy), "Pressed" (layered), and "Changed" (banded) rocks. 
- **Constitutional Link**: *Quantum Darwinism* - Truth is what survives. Rocks are the surviving record of the earth's history.

### Day 2: The Earth's Skin (Soil Layers)
- **Indoor (Agent Thea)**: **The Soil Profile Jar.** Layer Sand, Silt, and Clay in a jar with water. Watch them settle into distinct layers.
- **Outdoor (Agent Scout)**: **The Dirt Dig.** Use trowels to dig a small pit (6 inches deep). Observe the color change from the top (Humus) to the bottom (Subsoil). Look for "The Architects" (worms).
- **Constitutional Link**: *Circularity* - Dead leaves become topsoil, which feeds the next generation of trees.

### Day 3: The Great Transformation (The Rock Cycle)
- **Indoor (Agent Thea)**: **Crayon Rock Cycle.** Use crayon shavings to simulate the cycle: Pressure (squeezing shavings = Sedimentary), Heat (melting = Igneous), and Squishing (Metamorphic).
- **Outdoor (Agent Scout)**: **Erosion Observation.** Find a slope or a wash-out area after rain. Observe how water moves the "skin" of the earth.
- **Constitutional Link**: *Adaptation* - Even the bones of the earth (rocks) change over time to fit the environment.

### Day 4: Hidden Treasures (Minerals & Crystals)
- **Indoor (Agent Thea)**: **Hardness Test.** Use the Mohs Scale (fingernail vs. penny vs. nail) to see which rocks are "strongest."
- **Outdoor (Agent Scout)**: **The Sun Sparkle Hunt.** Use magnifying glasses to find tiny crystals or "sparkles" in local rocks. Discuss how these are the "Seeds of the Earth."
- **Constitutional Link**: *Intrinsic Value of Life* - Minerals have value as the foundation of all biological life.

### Day 5: Shaping the Land (Landforms)
- **Indoor (Agent Thea)**: **Landform Matching.** Match cards (Mountain, Valley, Canyon, Delta) to a large scenic poster.
- **Outdoor (Agent Scout)**: **Mini-Erosion Experiment.** Build a "Hill" in the mud kitchen. Use a watering can to simulate a "storm." Watch the mountains become valleys and the deltas form at the bottom.
- **Constitutional Link**: *Interconnectedness* - The rain (Water) shapes the earth (Geology), which decides where the trees grow (Ecology).

## III. Visual Aid Specifications

### 1. Rock Type Classification Chart (V7)
- **Type**: 3-Column Wall Chart
- **Content**: 
    - **Igneous** (Icon: Volcano)
    - **Sedimentary** (Icon: Stacking Bricks)
    - **Metamorphic** (Icon: Squeezing Hands)
- **Purpose**: To provide a visual "Control of Error" for rock sorting.

### 2. Simplified Rock Cycle Diagram (V8)
- **Type**: Circular Flowchart
- **Content**: A loop showing: Melting -> Cooling -> Weathering -> Compacting -> Heat/Pressure.
- **Purpose**: To teach that the earth is in constant transformation.

### 3. Landform Infographic (V9)
- **Type**: Large Scenic Poster
- **Content**: A unified landscape showing a Mountain leading to a Valley, a Canyon carved by a River, and a Delta at the Ocean.
- **Purpose**: To connect geological processes to the big picture of the landscape.
