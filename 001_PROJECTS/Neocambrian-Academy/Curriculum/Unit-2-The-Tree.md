# Unit 2: The Tree
**Theme**: Anatomy, Energy (Photosynthesis), and Habitats.

## Objectives
- Identify the four primary parts: Root, Trunk, Branch, Leaf/Needle.
- Understand "How the Tree Eats" (Capillary action and Sunlight).
- Differentiate between Deciduous ("Naked in Winter") and Evergreen ("Always has Clothes").

## Activities
- **Indoor**: Celery/food coloring capillary action experiment.
- **Outdoor**: Anchor Tree habitat search (spiderwebs, nests).

## Visual Aids
- Parts of the Tree 3-Part Cards.
- Simplified Photosynthesis Chart.
- Root System Diagram (The Hidden Half).
