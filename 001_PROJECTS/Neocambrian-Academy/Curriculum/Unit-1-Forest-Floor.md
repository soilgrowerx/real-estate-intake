# Unit 1: The Forest Floor
**Theme**: Decomposition, Worms, and the Mycelial Web.

## Objectives
- Understand the role of "Soil Alchemy" (mixing soil, water, and organic matter).
- Identify decomposition in action (the "Decomposition Station").
- Recognize the interconnectedness of the forest floor (worms and roots).

## Activities
- **Indoor**: Sorting soil samples (clay, sand, loam).
- **Outdoor**: Worm tunnel hunt and Micro Habitat observation.

## Visual Aids
- Soil Profile Diagram (Topsoil, Subsoil, Parent Material).
- Worm Life Cycle Card.
