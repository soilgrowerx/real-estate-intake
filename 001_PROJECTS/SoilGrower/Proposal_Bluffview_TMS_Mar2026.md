## Project Overview
Specialized soil invigoration, nutrient management, and top-dressing for 20 preserved trees across a 12,000 square foot area at the Bluffview development site.
Based on TAMU soil analysis (Report 691815), the scope corrects a moderately alkaline pH of 7.8 and addresses severe phosphorus (12 ppm deficit) and nitrogen deficits.

- **Investment:** $35,055.00
