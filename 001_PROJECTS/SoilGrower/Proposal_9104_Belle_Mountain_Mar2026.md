## Project Overview
Following our site walk, the primary goal for your property is to relieve soil compaction, correct root flare issues, and introduce a permanent, regenerative soil architecture to support the long-term health and resilience of your oaks. The thinning canopy observed is a common stress response to recent weather extremes, but we can significantly boost their vitality by focusing on the soil.

## Scope of Work

**1. Deep Root Soil Invigoration (Airspade & Biochar Integration)**
*   **Target Trees:** 6 Live Oaks (Front Yard), 2 Live Oaks (South Side), 2 Shin Oaks (Pool Area).
*   **Process:** We will use an airspade to safely excavate 4-8 inch pockets around the critical root zones without damaging existing roots or irrigation lines. 
*   **Application:** We will pack these pockets with our **Symbiosis TX Custom Regenerative Biochar**.
*   **Root Flare:** We will perform minor root flare exposure on trees where soil is piled too high, removing constricting roots to allow proper vascular flow.

**2. Manual Biochar Application (Back Property)**
*   **Target Trees:** Several Red Oaks in the back.
*   **Process:** We will manually work the biochar blend into the soil by hand to support their root systems.

**3. Hazard Mitigation**
*   **Target:** 1 struggling Red Oak.
*   **Process:** We recommend the complete removal of this tree. In its current state, it acts as a primary entry point for beetles that transmit Oak Wilt, posing a significant risk to the healthy oaks on your property. *(Removal cost can be quoted separately or wrapped into this scope if desired).*
