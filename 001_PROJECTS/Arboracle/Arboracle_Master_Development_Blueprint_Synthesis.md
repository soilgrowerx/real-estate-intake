## 🌟 Vision: The Agent Swarm & Dendritic Management
Arboracle is transitioning from a static application into a **decentralized swarm of intelligent AI agents**. This architecture is grounded in **Dendritic Forest Management (DFM)**, mirroring nature's optimal resource distribution and connectivity.

### Core Principles:
- **Dendritic Design:** Branching, network-based structures replace isolated data silos.
- **Agent-Ready APIs:** Stateless endpoints allowing agents to modify single data points without heavy overhead.
- **Voice-First Structures:** Data models designed for natural language field updates.
- **Asynchronous Flow:** Non-blocking handling of iNaturalist sync and transcription.
