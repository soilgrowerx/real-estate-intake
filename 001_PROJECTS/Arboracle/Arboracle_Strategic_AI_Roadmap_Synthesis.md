- **Capabilities:** AI-powered species ID, automated estimates, and basic health reports.
- **Goal:** Move from 20-minute field assessments to 2-minute "voice-first" captures.

### **Horizon 2: The Proactive Guide (Bodhi Integration)**
- **Focus:** Semantic context and pattern recognition.

### **Horizon 3: The Autonomous Steward (Endgame)**

- **Capabilities:** Integration with semi-autonomous field assistants (drones/humanoids) for soil sampling and precision treatment.
- **Goal:** Planetary-scale carbon and biodiversity monitoring as a self-sustaining infrastructure.
