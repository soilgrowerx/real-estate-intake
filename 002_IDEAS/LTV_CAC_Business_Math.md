# LTV:CAC - Business as a Math Problem

**Date:** 2026-03-28
**Tags:** #business #hormozi #economics
**MOC:** [[002_IDEAS]]

## Core Thesis
Business is a math problem defined by the LTV (Lifetime Value) to CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost) ratio.

- **LTV:** Gross profit per customer x lifespan of relationship
- **CAC:** Total spend to acquire one customer

### Automation Benchmarks
- Fully automated: 3:1 minimum
- Two components automated: 6:1
- One component automated: 9:1
- Fully manual: 12:1

### Three Key Moves
1. **Nail the Offer (Raise LTV):** Stop competing on price. Bundle premium value.
2. **Lower CAC with Content:** Use value-driven content to build trust and reduce ad dependency.
3. **Accelerate Cash Flow:** Require upfront payments, reinvest in ads.

### Application to the Garden (Soil Grower ecosystem)
- Offer: Regenerative land outcomes + trackable tech stack.
- LTV Multiplier: Long-term stewardship contracts.
- CAC Reduction: Open-source community (stim.bio) and public content.
- Cash Flow: Upfront deposits on land management contracts.
