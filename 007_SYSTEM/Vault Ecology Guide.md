# 🌿 Neocambrian Vault Ecology: The Neural PIPR Framework

Welcome to the **Neocambrian Garden**. This vault is no longer a static library; it is a **Biosynthetic Mycelium**. It is designed to bridge biological wisdom and digital intelligence through a flattened, high-voltage neural network.

## 🌳 Core Axioms of the Digital Garden

1.  **Second Brain Primacy (The Anchor)**: Obsidian is the definitive source of truth. All project data, research, and strategies are anchored here and symlinked to the active workspace. This ensures 200-year portability.
2.  **PIPR Structure (The Architecture)**: We organize by **Projects, Ideas, People, and Resources**. This aligns our internal data with the Council's routing logic.
3.  **Neural Flattening (The End of Hierarchy)**: We reject deep folder nests ("Hierarchy Friction"). Files live in flattened roots and are categorized by **Domain Tags** and **Maps of Content (MOCs)**.
4.  **The Blood Signature (Lived Intelligence)**: We prioritize the *emotional tone* and *environmental context* of voice notes and field observations over literal machine transcription. Data is grounded in raw, lived experience.
5.  **The Heartbeat Protocol (Telemetry)**: Our digital agents pulse hourly against the `NORTH_STAR.md` to ensure every token advances our primary missions.

## 🏞️ The PIPR Habitats (Folder Structure)

*   **`000_INBOX/`**: The Spore Intake. Fertile soil for new seeds and raw capture (WhatsApp, voice notes, clippings).
*   **`001_PROJECTS/`**: The Active Forests. Work with a deadline and a specific "Sniper Shot" objective (e.g., `Lumbee Sovereignty`, `Arboracle PHC`).
*   **`002_IDEAS/`**: The Sprouting Ground. Creative sparks, speculative research, and "Shadow Fork" experiments that haven't yet become projects.
*   **`003_PEOPLE/`**: The Social Graph. Flattened records of family, mentors, tribes, and contacts. Every person is a first-class entity.
*   **`004_RESOURCES/`**: The Mycelial Library. A **flattened** root containing all evergreen knowledge. No sub-folders. Categorization is handled via `#domain/` tags.
*   **`005_JOURNAL/`**: The Daily Pulse. Biological respiratory cycles, daily logs, and "In-breath/Out-breath" regulation.
*   **`006_ARCHIVES/`**: The Compost. Finished projects and static history (e.g., `Scholarship/Static_OSU`). Valuable nutrients for future generations.
*   **`007_SYSTEM/`**: The OS Kernel. Templates, Scripts, the **`SYMLINK_REGISTRY.md`**, and the **`bootstrap.sh`** seed script.

## 🦋 Neural Categorization: Tags & MOCs

1.  **Domain Tags**: Use `#domain/[topic]` (e.g., `#domain/forestry`, `#domain/arboracle`) to define the niche. This replaces sub-folders.
2.  **MOCs (Maps of Content)**: Located in `007_SYSTEM/MOCS/`. These are the "Table of Contents" for your neural network. A single note can live in multiple MOCs simultaneously.
3.  **Status Tags**: 
    *   `#spore` (Raw intake)
    *   `#sprout` (Developing idea)
    *   `#bloom` (Active project)
    *   `#harvest` (Completed/Archived)

## 💓 The Heartbeat Protocol: Operational Rhythms

*   **Hourly Telemetry**: Agents wake up and check the `007_SYSTEM/NORTH_STAR.md`. 
    *   *Question:* "What have I done in the last hour to move the needle on our 200-year legacy?"

*   **The Shadow Fork (Rebuild)**: Regularly audit the `SYMLINK_REGISTRY.md` to ensure the system can be "compiled" by your descendants in one command.

## 🛡️ Sovereign Maintenance

*   **Pruning**: Use **Goose** or **Pi** to identify "Dead Weight" (oddly named files, dead links, outdated research) and move them to `006_ARCHIVES/System_Cleanup/`.
*   **Hardening**: Ensure all credential folders and sensitive notes are locked with `700` permissions.
