# Typical School Calendar Days

**USER**:
How many days are in a typical grade school calendar year? 

---

**ASSISTANT**:
A typical grade school calendar year in the United States usually consists of **180 days**. However, this can vary slightly depending on the state or school district, with some ranging from 170 to 190 days. Schools often follow a schedule that includes breaks for holidays, winter and spring vacations, and teacher workdays or professional development days.
