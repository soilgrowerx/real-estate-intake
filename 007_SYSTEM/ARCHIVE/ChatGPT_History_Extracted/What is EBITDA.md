# What is EBITDA?

**USER**:
What is EBITDA?

---

**ASSISTANT**:
EBITDA stands for Earnings Before Interest, Taxes, Depreciation, and Amortization. It&#x27;s a financial metric often used to evaluate a company&#x27;s operating performance. By removing the effects of financing, accounting, and tax decisions, EBITDA aims to show a purer picture of how well a business is generating cash from its operations.

Philosophically, EBITDA is like the &quot;heartbeat&quot; of a company. It helps you understand the core vitality of a business, much like how the essence of martial arts isn&#x27;t just the moves but the spirit and skill underlying them.
