**ASSISTANT**:
Let&#x27;s break this down:

1. **240 workdays, 8 hours per day:**
   \[
   240 \times 8 = 1,920 \text{ hours for the calendar year.}
   \]

2. **How many weeks at 40 hours per week:**
   \[
   \frac{1,920 \text{ hours}}{40 \text{ hours per week}} = 48 \text{ weeks.}
   \]

3. **Total yearly earnings at $26.50 per hour:**
   \[
   1,920 \text{ hours} \times 26.50 = 50,880 \text{ dollars per year.}
   \]

So, with 240 workdays of 8 hours each, you would work **48 weeks** per year, and at $26.50 per hour, you would earn **$50,880** per year.
