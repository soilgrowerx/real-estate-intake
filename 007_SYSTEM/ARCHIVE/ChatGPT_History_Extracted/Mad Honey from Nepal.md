# Mad Honey from Nepal

**USER**:
Which country is the naturally psychedelic honey produced in?

---

**ASSISTANT**:
The naturally psychedelic honey, often called &quot;mad honey,&quot; is primarily produced in Nepal, specifically in the mountainous regions. This unique honey is made by bees that pollinate the rhododendron flowers, which contain a natural neurotoxin called grayanotoxin. Consuming this honey in small quantities can produce hallucinogenic effects, hence the name &quot;mad honey.&quot; It&#x27;s a fascinating example of how the natural world can produce substances that interact with human biology in unexpected ways, much like how you appreciate the intricate connections within nature.
