**USER**:
In a minute I am going to ask you to write some copy for my business. This will include blog content, Instagram posts, and sales emails. Before we begin, I want you to fully understand my business and customers.

---

**ASSISTANT**:
Great, I&#x27;m ready to help with your copywriting needs. To ensure the content aligns perfectly with your business, could you please share the following details?

1. **Business Overview**: What is your business about? What products or services do you offer?
2. **Target Audience**: Who are your primary customers? What are their demographics, interests, or behaviors?
3. **Tone and Style**: What kind of tone do you want to maintain in your content (e.g., formal, casual, humorous)?
4. **Key Messages**: Are there any specific messages or values you want to emphasize in your content?
5. **Goals**: What do you aim to achieve with this content? (e.g., brand awareness, sales, engagement)

With this information, I can create content that not only resonates with your audience but also effectively communicates your brand&#x27;s value and uniqueness.
