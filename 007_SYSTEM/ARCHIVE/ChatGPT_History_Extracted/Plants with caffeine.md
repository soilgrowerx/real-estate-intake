# Plants with caffeine.

**USER**:
List all plants that produce caffeine

---

**ASSISTANT**:
There are several plants that produce caffeine, including:

1. Coffee (Coffea arabica, Coffea robusta)
2. Tea (Camellia sinensis)
3. Yerba mate (Ilex paraguariensis)
4. Guarana (Paullinia cupana)
5. Yaupon holly (Ilex vomitoria)
6. Cola nut (Cola acuminata, Cola nitida)
7. Guayusa (Ilex guayusa) 

Note that caffeine can also be synthetically produced and added to certain foods, beverages, and medications.
