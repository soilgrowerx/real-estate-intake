   - **Pipe Blockages:** There might be a blockage in the distribution pipes leading to uneven distribution of effluent.
   - **Physical Damage:** The dog&#x27;s digging might have damaged a pipe or disrupted the soil structure.

  - **Potential Issues:** Simply adding soil might not resolve underlying saturation or blockages.

- **Planting Grass and Wildflowers:**
  - **Benefits:** Vegetation can help absorb excess moisture.
  - **Caution:** Avoid deep-rooted plants that could infiltrate pipes.

1. **Professional Inspection:**

   - **Soil Evaluation:** A percolation test might be necessary to assess soil absorption capabilities.

2. **Water Usage Management:**
   - **Conservation:** Reduce water usage to lessen the load on the septic system.

5. **Protect the Leach Field:**
   - **Limit Traffic:** Keep heavy machinery and vehicles off the area to prevent compaction.
   - **Proper Cover:** Use grass instead of impermeable surfaces to allow for aeration.

- **System Overload:**
  - The septic system might be undersized for your household&#x27;s needs.
