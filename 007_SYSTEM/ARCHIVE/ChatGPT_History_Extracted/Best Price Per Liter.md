# Best Price Per Liter

**USER**:
If a gallon is $75 and a quart is $25 what is the best price for a liter? 

---

**ASSISTANT**:
First, let&#x27;s convert gallons and quarts to liters to determine which has a better price per liter:

1 gallon = 3.78541 liters, so:
- The price per liter for a gallon at $75 is:  
  \[ \frac{75}{3.78541} \approx 19.81 \text{ per liter} \]

1 quart = 0.946353 liters, so:
- The price per liter for a quart at $25 is:  
  \[ \frac{25}{0.946353} \approx 26.42 \text{ per liter} \]

### Conclusion:
The gallon offers a better price per liter at $19.81 compared to the quart&#x27;s $26.42. Therefore, the best price for a liter would be $19.81.
