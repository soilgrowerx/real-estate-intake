It&#x27;s a makeshift arrangement used in emergencies or as a short-term fix. Define axon

---

**ASSISTANT**:
An axon is a long, slender projection of a nerve cell, or neuron, that typically conducts electrical impulses away from the neuron&#x27;s cell body. It&#x27;s the primary transmission line of the nervous system. The function of the axon is to transmit information to different neurons, muscles, and glands. Some axons can be quite long, extending over a meter in humans, while others can be very short. Many axons are covered with a layer of myelin sheath, which acts as an insulator and increases the speed of nerve impulse transmission.
