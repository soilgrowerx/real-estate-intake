As for your question about the &quot;venture path,&quot; it generally refers to a strategic outline of how a business plans to evolve from its current state to achieving its future objectives. This could include elements like market entry strategies, scaling plans, revenue models, and key milestones. It’s less detailed than a full business plan but more focused on growth trajectory and major strategic steps needed to reach specific goals. A venture path could form a critical part of a business pitch or investor presentation to demonstrate clarity of vision and operational foresight.
